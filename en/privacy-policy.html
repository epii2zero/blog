Privacy Policy

Draft date: 2026-04-16

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how the AI News blog ("we", "the Blog") collects, uses, stores, shares, and deletes personal data. Users may review this Policy any time by visiting the footer link or interacting with the cookie consent banner.

This Policy is designed to comply with:

  • Republic of Korea Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA), particularly Articles 15, 17, and 22
  • Republic of Korea Act on Promotion of Information and Communications Network Utilization and Information Protection
  • EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR, Regulation 2016/679) — when accessed from the EU/EEA
  • U.S. FTC Endorsement Guides (affiliate disclosure covered under a separate policy)

2. Data We Collect

We collect data in two tiers based on the user's consent. Cookie specifics are listed in the cookie details below.

2-1. Strictly necessary (no consent required, basic site operation)

  • Access IP address (server logs, retained up to 30 days)
  • User-Agent, Referer, request path (server logs, up to 30 days)
  • Strictly-necessary session cookies (functional and security: e.g., language preference, CSRF token)

2-2. Optional (activated only after explicit user consent)

Analytics cookies— Google Analytics 4 ("GA4")

  • _ga (domain-level visitor identifier, default retention 2 years)
  • _ga_<container-id> (session state, default retention 2 years)
  • Aggregated pageviews, events, screen resolution, approximate location (city level)
  • GA4 event retention is configured to 14 months (shorter than GA4's maximum)

Advertising cookies — Google AdSense

  • __gads, __gpi (ad personalization and frequency capping, per Google policy)
  • Ad impressions and click events, device identifiers, IP-based approximate location

Declining optional cookies does not restrict core Blog access.

4. Retention and Deletion

  • Server access logs: auto-deleted after 30 days
  • GA4 event data: retained for 14 months then auto-deleted (GA4 setting)
  • AdSense cookies: per Google's policy (typically up to 13 months; see Google Cookie Policy)
  • When a user deletes cookies or withdraws consent, affected cookies become void immediately; previously collected aggregated data remains in anonymized form for the retention windows above.

6. Consent and Withdrawal — Cookie Banner

  • On first visit, a cookie consent banner is displayed. Optional cookies (analytics, advertising) are activated only after explicit user consent.
  • We implement Google Consent Mode v2. Before consent, ad_storage, analytics_storage, ad_user_data, and ad_personalization all remain denied.
  • Withdrawal paths:
    1. Re-open the consent banner any time via the "Cookie settings" link in the footer and re-select categories
    2. Delete or block cookies in your browser
    3. Disable personalized ads in Google Ad Settings

7. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete their personal data
  • Object to processing or withdraw consent
  • GDPR subjects: additionally the rights to data portability, restriction of processing, and objection to automated decision-making

Exercise these rights via the Contact channel in §10. Requests are processed within 10 business days.

8. Security Measures

  • TLS 1.2+ encryption for data in transit
  • Administrative access separation and access logging
  • Encrypted server log storage, auto-purged after 30 days

9. Children's Privacy

This Blog is not directed to children under 14. If we learn that a user under 14 has submitted personal data, we delete it immediately.
